Vault of β — Tower Defense

Protect the 20% Vault of β from Paper Hands, Tax Men, and Exit Liquidity enjoyers.

Vault HP
100%
Copium 50
Wave 1 / 5
Towers
Tips

1. Select a tower, then click a glowing slot near the lane.

2. Defeated enemies drop Copium for more defenses.

3. If they reach the Vault, HP drops. At 0% you’re rekt.

Enemy Types

  • Paper Hand Peasants — fast, low HP, chip away at the Vault.
  • Tax Man — slower, tougher, chunks HP if he reaches the Vault.

Vault Lore

20% of the GCAβ supply rests inside the Vault of β — a glowing treasury of unspent chaos. If the Paper Hands breach it, the meme collapses into a sad red candle.

Survive all waves and the Vault holds. Lose, and the cult of β will say: “skill issue.”

Controls

  • Click tower buttons to select which β-defense to place.
  • Click glowing slots (above/below the path) to build.
  • Press “Start Wave” when you’re ready.
